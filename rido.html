How Rido works

Four simple steps — whether you're riding or driving.

Download & sign up

Create your account as a Rider or Captain and set your route preferences.

Match on route

Riders search a destination; captains see requests on their active route.

Verify & ride

Captain enters the Ride OTP, trip starts, and both sides track live on the map.

Complete & receipt

Mark the trip done, share fuel cost, and download your PDF receipt.
